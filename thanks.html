SOM
Founded by the Hero Group, BML Munjal University's School of Management is engaged in creating, preserving & imparting internationally benchmarked knowledge & skills to a diverse community of students. The school has been founded with the objective of transforming management education in India by creating a world-class teaching & learning environment.

Imperial College London is playing a mentoring role in the formation & operation of the School of Management. It is closely involved in a wide spectrum of activities such as curriculum design, content development, student exchange, summer study programmes, joint research activities, digital learning platforms, faculty training, educational outreach and executive education programmes.

We offer the following programmes
• MBA
• MBA in Accounting and Finance
• MBA in Business Analytics (in association with IBM)
